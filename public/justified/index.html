About Justified

Justified is an American crime drama television series that premiered on March 16, 2010, on the FX network. The show was developed by Graham Yost and is based on Elmore Leonard's novels Pronto and Riding the Rap, and his short story "Fire in the Hole".

The series stars Timothy Olyphant as Deputy U.S. Marshal Raylan Givens, a tough and dedicated lawman with a strong moral code. The show is set in Kentucky and offers a modern-day Western feel, with elements of crime, drama, and dark humor.

Justified received critical acclaim throughout its six-season run, with praise for its acting, writing, and character development. The show has garnered a dedicated fan base and is often regarded as one of the best television dramas of its time.
